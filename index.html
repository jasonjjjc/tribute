Robin Williams

Robin was a legendary actor, never in the news for the wrong reasons, who brought love, laughter and life everywhere he went, except for at home. He is the quintessential paragon of a person who devoted years to the service of others by making people laugh until they cried, all whilst hiding demons and negativity privately held. Join us as we explore the greatest comedian of the past 100 years and discover why he stands out amongst all the legends as the single most irreplacable persona that Hollywood ever produced.
